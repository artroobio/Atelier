Design System: L'Atelier Transparent

This system is designed to maintain the high-end, minimalist aesthetic of the "Architectural Glass Hero" section while ensuring the rest of the website feels structurally sound and visually clear.

1. Core Color Palette

The Neutral Base (The "Concrete")

Primary Base: #F2F2F2 (Soft Concrete) - Use for main section backgrounds. It provides a softer, more premium feel than pure white.

Secondary Base: #EBEBEB (Cool Grey) - Use for alternating sections to create subtle structural depth.

Dark Base: #1A1A1A (Obsidian) - Use for the footer and high-impact "intermission" sections.

Typography & Ink

Primary Text: #000000 (Pure Black) - Used for headers and main navigation to maintain maximum contrast.

Body Text: #333333 (Deep Charcoal) - Used for long-form paragraphs to reduce eye strain.

Metadata/Taglines: #666666 (Steel Grey) - For dates, labels, and secondary information.

The "Glass" Accents

Refraction Blue: #D4E6F1 (Glass Tint) - Use sparingly as a subtle background glow or hover state to mimic the edge of a thick glass pane.

Frosted Surface: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.3) - The primary material for cards and UI components over images.

2. Typography System

Headlines (H1, H2): Playfair Display (Regular 400 / Italic 400). Letter-spacing: -1px or -2px for a compressed, editorial look.

Navigation & Buttons: Inter (Semi-Bold 600). Text-transform: uppercase. Letter-spacing: 2px to 4px.

Body Copy: Inter (Light 300). Line-height: 1.6. This ensures the "airiness" of the brand.

3. Materiality & Interaction Rules

The "Border" Rule: All glass elements should have a 1px solid rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.5) border to simulate the physical edge of glass.

The "Motion" Rule: Use GSAP for all section reveals. Elements should "emerge" from a blur rather than just fading in.

The "Stagger" Rule: Avoid perfect vertical alignment in project galleries. Offset images and text to mimic architectural blueprints.

4. Suggested Section Flow

Hero Section: (Existing) Structure & Transparency.

The Philosophy: Large typography section over a semi-transparent background.

Selected Works: A refractive grid of project thumbnails with glassmorphism hover states.

The Process: A horizontal scroll section showing the journey from "Sketch to Structure."

The Archive: A list-based view of all projects using the bold typography style of your menu.

Contact/Footer: High-contrast Obsidian section with minimal white text.
